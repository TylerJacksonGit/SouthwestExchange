About Southwest Exchange

At Southwest Exchange, we specialize in sourcing and supplying precious metals, historic currency, and unique collectibles. What started as a personal interest in the heritage of the American Southwest has grown into a dedicated operation across eBay, Whatnot, and Mercari.

Our focus is straightforward: providing collectors with authentic items, transparent service, and secure, reliable shipping.

Our Inventory

We maintain a rotating selection of high-quality items for both investors and hobbyists:

  • Bullion & Precious Metals: Gold, silver, and copper pieces, ranging from standard 1oz Indian Head designs to limited mintage and colorized rounds.
  • Historic Currency: A curated selection of vintage paper money, including Silver Certificates ($1 1935/1957), Federal Reserve Star Notes, and Red Seal notes.
  • Pop Culture & Modern Numismatics: Modern 1oz silver collectibles and themed chibi coins for fans and contemporary collectors.
  • Antiques & Regional Finds: A diverse mix of regional antiques and unique apparel sourced from across the Southwest.

The Exchange Community

Whether you’re bidding in our live Whatnot auctions or browsing our eBay listings, we prioritize a professional and efficient experience. Every item we sell is handled with care, ensuring that your investment—whether it’s your first ounce of copper or a rare star note—arrives exactly as described.
